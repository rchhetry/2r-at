Certifications

2R-AT is dedicated to maintaining the highest industry standards for quality, security, and privacy. Our commitment is reflected in the following certifications and compliance frameworks we adhere to:

SOC 2 Type II

Description: This report provides a detailed assessment of our organization's controls relevant to security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy of information. A Type II report includes management's assertion on the effectiveness of the controls over a period of time.

Benefit: Offers independent assurance of our robust information security practices and operational effectiveness, particularly for safeguarding client data.

ISO 27001 (Information Security Management System)

Description: This internationally recognized standard specifies the requirements for establishing, implementing, maintaining, and continually improving an information security management system (ISMS). It provides a systematic approach to managing sensitive company information so that it remains secure.

Benefit: Demonstrates our commitment to a comprehensive and systematic approach to managing information security risks and protecting client data.

NIST Compliant

Description: We align our cybersecurity frameworks and practices with the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) guidelines, including the NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) and other relevant special publications (e.g., NIST SP 800-53).

Benefit: Ensures our security controls are based on widely accepted industry best practices and provides a robust, risk-based approach to cybersecurity.

CISSP (Certified Information Systems Security Professional)

Description: Many of our senior security strategists and architects hold this globally recognized certification, validating their expertise in designing, engineering, implementing, and managing an organization's overall information security program.

Benefit: Ensures our strategic and architectural security guidance is provided by highly qualified and experienced professionals.

CISM (Certified Information Security Manager)

Description: Our leadership team includes CISM-certified professionals, demonstrating their proven ability to manage, design, oversee, and assess an enterprise's information security.

Benefit: Guarantees strong governance and management oversight in our cybersecurity operations and client engagements.

OSCP (Offensive Security Certified Professional)

Description: Our Principal Offensive Engineers are OSCP-certified, proving their practical, hands-on penetration testing skills against various systems and networks.

Benefit: Confirms our red team's capability to identify real-world vulnerabilities through advanced, ethical hacking techniques.

GCTI (GIAC Certified Threat Intelligence)

Description: Our Cyber Intelligence team is equipped with GCTI certification, signifying expertise in collecting, analyzing, and disseminating actionable threat intelligence.

Benefit: Ensures our proactive threat analysis and intelligence gathering are conducted with cutting-edge methodologies.

Multi-Cloud Security Certified (AWS, Azure, GCP)

Description: Our Chief Cloud Architect and cloud security specialists hold multiple certifications from leading cloud providers, validating their proficiency in securing complex cloud environments.

Benefit: Guarantees expert design and implementation of secure solutions across diverse cloud platforms.

GDPR & CCPA Compliance

Description: While not a certification, we operate in full compliance with global data protection regulations such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), ensuring robust data privacy practices.

Benefit: Demonstrates our commitment to protecting personal data and adhering to international privacy laws.

These certifications and compliances underscore 2R-AT's dedication to delivering secure, compliant, and high-quality cybersecurity services.
